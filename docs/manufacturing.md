# IoT in Manufacturing

IoT is enhancing manufacturing processes by enabling real-time monitoring and automation.

## Predictive Maintenance

- **Equipment Monitoring**: Monitor equipment health in real-time.
- **Failure Prediction**: Predict equipment failures before they occur.

## Asset Tracking

- **Location Tracking**: Track the location of assets in real-time.
- **Condition Monitoring**: Monitor the condition of assets.

## Quality Control

- **Automated Inspections**: Perform automated quality inspections.
- **Data Analytics**: Analyze production data to improve quality.
